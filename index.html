  • TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company. It is one of the largest IT services firms in the world and is part of the Tata Group, one of India's largest and oldest conglomerates. TCS was founded in 1968 and has since grown to become a global leader in the IT industry.

    Services: TCS offers a wide range of IT services, including software development, business process outsourcing (BPO), consulting, and more. Global Presence: TCS operates in numerous countries around the world, with offices and delivery centers in Asia, North America, Europe, and other regions. The company has a significant global workforce.

    As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) has a significant presence in Pune, India. Pune is one of the major IT hubs in India, and TCS has established multiple facilities in the city. Some key points about TCS in Pune: Development Centers: TCS has development centers in Pune where it engages in software development, testing, and other IT-related activities. Client Engagements: TCS Pune is involved in serving clients across different industries and geographies. The services offered include software development, consulting, business process outsourcing, and more.
